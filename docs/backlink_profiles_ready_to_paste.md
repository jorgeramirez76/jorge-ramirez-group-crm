## PRESS RELEASE (for PRLog, OpenPR, EIN Presswire)

**HEADLINE:** New Jersey Real Estate Team Launches AI-Powered Sales Platform to Revolutionize Agent Productivity

**SUBHEAD:** The Jorge Ramirez Group unveils AI Sales Pipeline, a self-hosted CRM that automates lead follow-up and appointment booking for real estate professionals.

**BODY:**

SUMMIT, NJ — The Jorge Ramirez Group, a top-producing real estate team at Keller Williams Premier Properties serving Central and Northern New Jersey, has launched AI Sales Pipeline (www.aisalespipeline.com), an AI-powered CRM platform designed specifically for real estate agents.

The platform combines conversational AI, automated email and text follow-up sequences, voice cloning technology, and intelligent pipeline management to help agents book more appointments and close more deals while reducing manual work.

"We built this because we were tired of paying hundreds of dollars a month for CRM tools that still required us to do all the follow-up ourselves," said George Ramirez, founder of The Jorge Ramirez Group. "With AI Sales Pipeline, the AI handles 90% of lead nurturing — texts, emails, even phone calls — so agents can focus on what matters: meeting clients and closing deals."

Key features include AI-powered lead follow-up trained on proven sales methodologies, personalized video generation for each lead, automated appointment booking, unified messaging across SMS, email, and iMessage, and a self-hosted architecture that keeps costs under $40 per month.

The Jorge Ramirez Group has sold over 500 homes across Summit, Short Hills, Chatham, and surrounding luxury markets over the past 15 years.

For more information, visit www.aisalespipeline.com or www.thejorgeramirezgroup.com.
